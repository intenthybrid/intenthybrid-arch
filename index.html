Signal, read, act.

For devs · feeds are ingested and normalized into a crowding index; the model reads positioning and returns a verdict against your thresholds; a verdict fires one of three outputs, an alert, a scoped agent call onchain, or a dashboard update. What it runs on.

        
A small stack with sharp edges: private inference, a crowding engine, pay-per-call settlement, and scoped onchain execution.

      

      

        
private inferenceprompts run on infrastructure you control, with no third-party prompt logging

        
crowding enginemarket and liquidation feeds normalize into a single crowding index per market

        
x402 settlementeach read is an HTTP endpoint priced per call, signed locally and settled onchain

        
scoped agentsagents execute onchain, bounded by caps, an allowlist, a slippage ceiling, and an expiry

        
local-first storagechat and history live in the browser; clearing it clears everything
